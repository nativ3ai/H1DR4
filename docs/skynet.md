# SKYNET

SKYNET is H1DR4's investigation and entity-profiling mode.

It is used to turn clues into structured profiles, map cues, wallet context, source links, and follow-up research vectors.

## What It Does

- Entity profiling from names, handles, emails, wallets, domains, IPs, and clues.
- Cross-source enrichment from public web, social context, wallet intelligence, and H1DR4 lookup tools.
- Map cues from IPs, addresses, cities, areas, and coordinates.
- Case-aware follow-up prompts.
- Tip drafting when findings are useful and source-backed.

## Agent Use

Agents can call SKYNET through MCP with `h1dr4_osint_agent`. Use it to enrich a case, not to fabricate allegations. Every serious claim should carry source links, confidence, and clear limitations.

## Safety

SKYNET is for public-interest intelligence and authorized research. Do not use it for harassment, credential abuse, unauthorized intrusion, or contacting private people.
